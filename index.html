  6. Crease along all the diagonals and along the centre.

  7. Turn the template over to the non coloured side. Add glue along the small side and fold in half along the centre line.

  8. Fold the left side under to create a mountain fold between the blue and red triangles.

  9. Fold under again between the blue and red triangles. This creates another mountain fold. Let the single layer flap sit on top of the existing paper.

  10. Now glue the two single layer flaps together to enclose all the uncoloured paper.

  11. Congratulations! You've just made a trihexaflexagon.
